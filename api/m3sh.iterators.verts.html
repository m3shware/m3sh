verts

m3sh.iterators.verts(obj)[source]

Vertex iterator.

The returned iterator traverses adjacent/incident vertices of obj depending on its type:

Vertex

↺ traversal of adjacent vertices

Face

↺ traversal of incident vertices

Mesh

in-order traversal of vertices
Parameters:

obj (Vertex or Face or Mesh) – The base object.

Yields:

Vertex

Note

When applied to a mesh, this iterator will skip any deleted vertices present in its vertex list.

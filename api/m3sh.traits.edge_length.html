edge_length

m3sh.traits.edge_length(item)[source]

Edge length statistics.

Minimal, maximal, and average edge length for a mesh or an individual face.

Parameters:

item (Face or Mesh) – Mesh or face of a mesh.

Returns:

  • min (float) – Minimal edge length.

  • max (float) – Maximal edge length.

  • avg (float) – Average edge length.
